Visual Design & Digital Arts

Illustration, Poster, Comic

2024 – 2025

This collection showcases a range of projects that blend creativity, technology, and purpose, bringing together conceptual artworks that explore creativity through various mediums and themes. These include the design from my fine art and GBDA courses such as thought-provoking collages, visual paradoxes, professionally designed logos, and comics that reveal the future of digital culture, among other works. Additionally, a major highlight was my emotional design project, a prototype of a lamp. These pieces reflect a thoughtful approach to design and artistic expression that blends imagination, technology, and storytelling—exploring the interplay between art, emotion, and function to engage the viewer on a deeper level.

"This video showcases a project in which I explored the concept of emotional design by creating a prototype that aims to evoke a specific emotional response during user interaction. At the heart of this work is a visually striking interactive table lamp that combines colorful lighting, soft textures, and reflective surfaces to evoke a sense of comfort, wonder, and serenity. By combining aesthetics, functionality, and emotional resonance, this work reflects my commitment to creating designs that resonate on a deeper level."
